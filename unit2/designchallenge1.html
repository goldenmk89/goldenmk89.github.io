How Do They Exhibit Effective Use of Space, Size, or Balance?

Bay Street BierGarten

Bay Street BierGarten uses size to establish a primary point of interes, or focal point. The viewer is immediately drawn to the words "Good Food, Craft Beer". The negative space puts visual emphasis on the positive space. The size of the focal point suggests the relative importance of the overall message the site is portraying. Lastly, the focal point is balanced center on the page, further drawing the viewers eye to that message.

Menagerie

The focal point in Menagerie is clearly emphasized through space, size, and balance. The size of the message "Climbing is for everyone" clearly distinguishes the focal point. It creates a hierarchy letting the viewer know the importance of the message. The negative space is creative and visually appealing, but does not take away from the positive space.

What Makes Them Similar and Different?

Similarities: The sites I have chosen use whitespace to frame a clear focal point. They make use of balance to portray the message intended for the viewer.

Differences: Menagerie creates more of a hierarchy to emphazise importance of objects on the page. Bay Street Biergarten uses white space pull the view to the focal point and emphazize their message.
